Returns Policy

    Returns Policy

    1. Eligibility for Returns:

    • Jerseys: You can initiate a return for soccer jerseys within 30 days from the date of delivery. The jerseys must be in unused condition, with all original tags and packaging intact.
    • Training Plans: You can request a refund for soccer training plans within 30 days of purchase, provided you haven't started or accessed the plan. If you've already accessed the plan or coaching services, the refund will not be applicable.

    2. Return Process for Soccer Jerseys:

    • Contact Customer Support: To initiate a return for a soccer jersey, please reach out to our customer support team through the "Contact Us" page or by emailing support@footgalaxy.com. Provide your order number, the item(s) you wish to return, and the reason for the return.
    • Return Authorization: Our customer support team will review your request and provide you with a return authorization (RA) number, along with further instructions on how to proceed.
    • Secure Packaging: Place the soccer jersey in a suitable package, ensuring it is well-protected to avoid damage during transit. Include a copy of the packing slip or order confirmation email with the return shipment.
    • Shipping: You are responsible for the shipping costs associated with returning the item. We recommend using a trackable and insured shipping method to ensure safe delivery.
    • Refund Process: Once we receive the returned jersey and verify its condition, we will process your refund within 5-7 business days. The refund will be issued to the original payment method used during the purchase.

    3. Return Process for Soccer Training Plans:

    • Cancellation: To initiate a return for a soccer training plan, contact our customer support team as mentioned above. We will verify your eligibility for a refund based on the plan's access history.
    • Refund Processing: If your request is eligible for a refund, we will process it within 5-7 business days. The refund will be issued to the original payment method used during the purchase.

    4. Exceptions to the Returns Policy:

    • Digital Products: Soccer training plans that have been accessed or used cannot be refunded, except in cases of technical issues or errors on our end.

    5. Contact Us:

    If you have any questions or need further assistance regarding our returns policy, please don't hesitate to contact our customer support team at support@footgalaxy.com or through the "Contact Us" page on our website.

    Please note that our returns policy is subject to change, and the terms in effect at the time of your purchase will apply.
