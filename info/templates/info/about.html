Welcome to Foot Galaxy!

We are a premier destination for all football enthusiasts, offering top-quality jerseys and great customer service. With our well-established store in London, we are dedicated to providing the best products and services to football lovers in the region.

Our History
  • Founded by passionate football aficionados, our journey began in the bustling city of London. Our founders, driven by their love for the sport and a vision to bring football enthusiasts together, embarked on a mission to create a one-stop destination for all things football-related. With a focus on quality, authenticity, and customer satisfaction, we quickly gained popularity and expanded our presence to cater to the ever-growing demand.
Our Stores
  • Our flagship store, located in the heart of London, on Oxford street, has been a hub for football fans seeking the latest, official jerseys of their favorite clubs and national teams. Boasting an extensive collection of football apparel, we take pride in offering genuine merchandise that allows you to wear your team's colors with pride.
